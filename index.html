What is Semantics?

Semantics simply means the study of meaning. It originates from the Greek word Sema which means Sign.

There are many ways we can describe meaning for a single word.

  • As a noun, "crane" can denote a type of large bird with long legs and neck. It can also refer to a piece of heavy machinery used for lifting and moving heavy objects.

What is an Ontology?

An ontology in the context of computer science and information science is a formal representation of knowledge as a set of concepts within a domain, and the relationships between those concepts.
